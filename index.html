About

Environments that
breathe. FX that hits.

I'm Gowtham Siddharth, a 3D environment and FX artist working across environment art, simulation, and animation.

I build worlds first — terrain, layout, mood — then bring them to life with fire, smoke, fluid, and cloth simulations, finished off with animation that gives a scene its final pulse.

Currently building Ronin, an original shinobi action-thriller, alongside freelance and studio-bound environment/FX work. Open to freelance projects and studio roles.
